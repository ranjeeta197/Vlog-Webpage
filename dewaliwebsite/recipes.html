Traditional Diwali Sweets & Snacks

Diwali is incomplete without delicious sweets and savory snacks. Here are some mouth-watering recipes you can try at home this festive season.

Popular Recipes

Ladoo

Sweet, round balls made from a variety of ingredients like chickpea flour, coconut, and semolina.

Kheer

A creamy dessert made from rice, milk, and sugar, flavored with cardamom and topped with nuts.

Samosa

A fried or baked pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and other fillings, served with chutney.

Barfi

A dense sweet made from condensed milk, sugar, and various flavors such as coconut, pistachio, or chocolate.
