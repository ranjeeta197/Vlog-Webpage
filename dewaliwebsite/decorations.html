Beautify Your Home with Diwali Decorations

Diwali is the perfect time to decorate your home with vibrant colors, lights, and flowers. From intricate rangoli designs to festive lights, decorations play a major role in the Diwali celebrations.

Types of Diwali Decorations

Rangoli

Rangoli is a traditional art form made with colored powders, flowers, or rice, designed on the floors of living rooms or courtyards. Itâ€™s believed to bring good luck and welcome the gods into the home.

Lighting Diyas and Lamps

Lighting diyas and electric lamps is a key aspect of Diwali. Homes are adorned with small earthen lamps to symbolize the triumph of light over darkness.

Flower Decorations

Flowers like marigolds and roses are commonly used to decorate homes. Garlands of flowers are hung at doorways, creating a welcoming and festive environment.
